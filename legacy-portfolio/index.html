About

Dr. Sanjay R. Chitnis is a distinguished academician and industry leader with over 30 years of experience. Currently serving as the Vice Chancellor of REVA University, he is a pioneer in implementing "AI-First" educational frameworks. With a background spanning global tech giants like Motorola and LG Electronics, he bridges the gap between deep-tech innovation and academic excellence. He holds a PhD in Machine Learning from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore.
